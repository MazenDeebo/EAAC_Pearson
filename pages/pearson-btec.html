What is Pearson BTEC?

Understanding the world's most trusted vocational education qualifications

About Pearson BTEC

BTEC (Business and Technology Education Council) qualifications are internationally-recognized higher education qualifications developed by Pearson, the world's leading learning company. These qualifications are designed in conjunction with employers, students, and professional bodies to ensure they meet real industry needs.

BTEC Higher Nationals are delivered at both universities and colleges worldwide, providing students with practical, hands-on learning experiences that directly relate to their chosen career paths.

Industry Engagement

At the heart of BTEC qualifications lies industry engagement, ensuring learners receive the most relevant career-related content. Employers are actively involved in:

  • Curriculum development and review
  • Assessment design and validation
  • Skills gap identification
  • Work placement opportunities

  • Immediate employment opportunities

Assessment & Student Support

Understanding how BTEC qualifications are assessed and supported

Assessment Methods

BTEC qualifications use assignment-based assessment rather than traditional exams, allowing students to demonstrate their knowledge and skills through:

  • Practical projects and case studies
  • Research assignments and reports
  • Presentations and group work
  • Portfolio development
  • Work-based learning activities

Grades are awarded as Pass, Merit, or Distinction based on the quality of work submitted.
