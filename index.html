About EAAC Group

Established in 1995, EAAC Training and Consulting Group has been a leading provider of vocational training and consultancy services in Egypt for over 29 years. We are passionate about equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge they need to thrive in dynamic fields.

We are proud to be an accredited center delivering Pearson BTEC Higher National Diploma (HND) and Higher National Certificate (HNC) qualifications. Pearson BTEC is globally recognized, ensuring our programs meet the highest standards and are valued by employers worldwide.

Pearson BTEC Qualifications

Globally recognized qualifications that bridge the gap between education and employment

Higher National Diploma (HND)

  • Level 5 qualification
  • 240 credits (2 years full-time)
  • Equivalent to first 2 years of bachelor's degree
  • Direct progression to final year of degree
  • Assignment-based assessment

Higher National Certificate (HNC)

  • Level 4 qualification
  • 120 credits (1 year full-time)
  • Foundation level engineering knowledge
  • Entry-level industry positions
  • Pathway to HND programs
