Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Pearson BTEC programs

A Higher National Diploma (HND) is a vocational qualification that typically takes two years of full-time study to complete. It equips individuals with a broad foundation in engineering principles and prepares them for various technician roles in their chosen field. HND programs combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills gained through workshops, lab work, and projects.

A Higher National Certificate (HNC) is a qualification similar to the HND but at a foundation level. An HNC program typically takes one year of full-time study and provides the essential knowledge and skills for entry-level positions in a specific engineering field.
